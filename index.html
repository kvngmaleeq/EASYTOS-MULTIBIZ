ABOUT US

EasyTos Global Multi Ventures is a trusted name in the hides and skins trade, specializing in air-dried cow hides (Ponmo). We are committed to ethical sourcing, hygienic drying processes, and excellent customer service. With operations spanning across East and West Africa, we are passionate about delivering quality at scale. Our permit-backed exports from Tanzania ensure our customers always receive authentic and pest-free products.
